About Us

DMV Math Circle is an initiative organized by talented and enthusiastic high school students from the DMV area who are committed to spreading their joy and passion for mathematics through a friendly and inclusive environment. Our staff members and community are a team of inspirational and highly qualified math competition veterans who have established their credibility through their numerous achievements in Math Olympiads (USA(J)MO, IMO) and participation in prestigious mathematics camps such as the Mathematics Olympiad Program (MOP). Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about DMV Math Circle.

What programs do you offer?

We offer a variety of programs including workshops, tutoring sessions, competitive programming, and math competitions. Our programs are designed to cater to students of all skill levels.

How can I join DMV Math Circle?

Joining DMV Math Circle is easy! Simply fill out our registration form on the website and choose the programs you are interested in. Our team will get in touch with you to provide further details.

Do you offer online resources?

Yes, we offer a wide range of online resources including practice problems, video tutorials, and interactive tools. Our online platform is accessible to all members and provides valuable support for both independent study and guided learning.

How can I contact DMV Math Circle?

You can contact us via email at dmvmathcircle@gmail.com or through our social media channels on Discord and Instagram. Our team is always ready to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have.
