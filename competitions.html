Competitions

DMV Math Circle is sending teams to various math competitions! These include:

MMATHS

MMATHS is a math competition that takes place annually, usually late October, in three different locations (UMich, UT Dallas, and Yale).

DMV Math Circle has sent teams of 6 people to MMATHS Yale in the past (2022, 2023). We will be sending teams of 6 to Yale* in 2024!

Stay tuned for more details as updates come out.

*Exceptions can be made (for example, if all 6 team members live near UMich).

PUMaC

PUMaC is a math competition hosted by Princeton University that takes place annually, usually mid November.

DMV Math Circle has sent teams of 8 people to PUMaC in the past (2023). We will be sending teams of 8 to PUMaC in 2024! If you choose to register, please let us know which 2 subject tests you wish to take! We encourage you to communicate with the rest of your team, as no more than 5 of the team members can take the same subject test (any more does not contribute to the overall team score).

Stay tuned for more details as updates come out.

CMIMC

CMIMC is a math competition hosted by Carnegie Mellon University that takes place annually, usually late March/early April.

DMV Math Circle has sent teams of 6 people to CMIMC in the past (2023, 2024). We will be sending teams of 6 to CMIMC in 2025!

Stay tuned for more details as updates come out.
